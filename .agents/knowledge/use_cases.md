# Strategic Use Cases: Ubot in Action

Ubot is designed to operate across three distinct domains: Personal, Front-Office, and Back-Office.

## 1. Personal Assistant (The Digital Secretary)

Ubot acts as an extension of the owner's executive function.

- **Scheduling**: Coordination between personal calendar and incoming requests.
- **Information Synthesis**: Summarizing long threads, emails, or research topics.
- **Memory Buffer**: Storing and retrieving personal facts, contact details, and preferences.

## 2. Front-Office Assistant (The Customer Interface)

Ubot handles external-facing interactions on behalf of a business or individual.

- **Lead Qualification**: Asking screening questions to potential clients via messaging channels.
- **FAQ Handling**: Providing instant, accurate answers based on the company's knowledge base.
- **escalation**: Intelligently routing complex queries to the owner (the human-in-the-loop).

## 3. Back-Office Assistant (The Automation Engine)

Ubot quietly orchestrates data and systems in the background.

- **Data Pipeline**: Monitoring incoming messages (e.g., invoices) and archiving them to the workspace.
- **System Monitoring**: Running periodic health checks and notifying the owner of anomalies.
- **Report Generation**: Aggregating data from multiple sources (CRM, Filesystem, Web) into a concise daily brief.

## 4. Case Study: The "Owner-Centric" Flow

1.  **Event**: A client messages the "Sales" WhatsApp number.
2.  **Ubot Action**: Qualifies the intent and checks the owner's availability.
3.  **Escalation**: Ubot reaches out to the owner's private chat: _"Someone is asking for a quote. I've drafted a response based on our rate sheet. Approve?"_
4.  **Completion**: Upon owner approval, Ubot sends the quote and creates a calendar invite.
